Mazdi Hasan

Co-Founder & Creative Visionary at Kathuray Furnishers

Birth: June 11, ####, Barisal, Bangladesh

Nationality: Bangladeshi

Education: Jahanara Israil School & College, Barisal

Role: Co-Founder

Known for: Innovative design practices at the company

About

Mazdi Hasan, born June 11 ####, co-founded Kathuray Furnishers in 2017 and has played a vital role in the company's growth since its inception. As a young Bangladeshi entrepreneur, he oversees multiple aspects of the business, including architectural design of retail locations, staff management, e-commerce operations, and international franchise development. Currently a student of Jahanara Israil School & College in Barisal, Mazdi has established himself as an innovative designer and strategic thinker in the furniture industry.

Journey

2014 - Early Beginnings

Enrolled for education at JISC Campus. Conceptualized initial showroom design for company

2018 - Staff Management

Implemented effective staff team management strategies

2021 - E-Commerce Expansion

Imposed digital marketing strategies to increase brand visibility of Kathuray Furnishers

2022 - Global Expansion

Took responsibility for the architectural design of company's retail locations

Career

• Mazdi was responsible for overseeing the management and design of our company’s social media operations and has earned numerous recognition due to his innovative strategies.

• During the pandemic, Kathuray Furnishers established a restaurant business, where Mazdi served as the head responsible for managing the customer support team, overseeing food inspections, and maintaining the restaurant's environment. However, the venture was later discontinued as the company shifted its focus towards developing other brands and expanding the parent company's branches.
