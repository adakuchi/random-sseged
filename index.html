About Kathuray Furnishers

Crafting Elegance Since 2017

Welcome to Kathuray Furnishers, a name synonymous with luxury and craftsmanship in Barishal Sadar, Bangladesh. Since 2017, we've been transforming homes with our exquisite furniture and decor, guided by the vision of our Chairman Karishma Hyder, Vice Chairman Mahethe Shuvo and Co-Founder Mazdi Hasan. Our passion lies in working with teak wood, crafting pieces that blend timeless elegance with contemporary design.

At Kathuray, we believe in merging innovation with tradition. Every item we create is a testament to superior quality and artistry, designed to elevate your living spaces.
